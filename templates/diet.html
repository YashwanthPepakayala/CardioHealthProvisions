Explore your Diet

Discover the Delicious Foods Essential for Nurturing Heart Health!



CHOLESTEROL

  • Increase Cholesterol

    • Saturated fats (found in red meat, full-fat dairy products, butter, and tropical oils like coconut and palm oil)
    • Trans fats (present in partially hydrogenated oils used in many processed and fried foods)
    • Processed and fried foods (including fast food, baked goods, and packaged snacks)
    • High-fat dairy products (such as cheese, cream, and whole milk)
    • Egg yolks (while eggs can be part of a healthy diet, the yolks are high in cholesterol)

  • Reduce Cholesterol

    • Oats and oat bran
    • Beans and legumes
    • Nuts (especially almonds, walnuts, and pistachios)
    • Fruits (such as apples, grapes, strawberries, and citrus fruits)
    • Vegetables (particularly leafy greens like spinach and kale, as well as broccoli and Brussels sprouts)
    • Fatty fish (such as salmon, mackerel, and trout)





BLOOD PRESSURE


  • Increase Blood Pressure

    • Sodium-rich foods: Canned soups, Processed meats (e.g., bacon, ham, sausage), Potato chips, Pickles, Cheese, Salted nuts
    • Hydration: Coconut water, Sports drinks (e.g., Gatorade), Herbal teas (e.g., chamomile, peppermint), Milk
    • Caffeine-containing beverages: Energy drinks, Soft drinks (e.g., cola), Dark chocolate (contains caffeine)
    • Nitrate-rich foods: Spinach, Arugula, Celery, Radishes, Carrots
    • Vitamin B12-rich foods: Shellfish (e.g., clams, mussels), Poultry (e.g., chicken, turkey), Fortified breakfast cereals, Fortified plant-based milk alternatives (e.g., almond milk, soy milk)
    • Healthy fats: Salmon, Flaxseeds, Chia seeds, Walnuts, Sunflower seeds

  • Reduce Blood Pressure

    • Leafy green vegetables: Spinach, Kale, Swiss chard, Collard greens, Romaine lettuce
    • Berries: Blueberries, Strawberries, Raspberries, Blackberries
    • Beets: Beetroot, Beet greens
    • Oats and whole grains: Oats (rolled oats, steel-cut oats, oat bran), Brown rice, Quinoa, Whole wheat, Barley
    • Fruits: Bananas, Pomegranates, Tomatoes
    • Fatty fish: Salmon, Mackerel, Trout, Sardines
    • Nuts and seeds: Almonds, Walnuts, Flaxseeds, Chia seeds
    • Low-fat dairy: Milk, Yogurt, Cheese (in moderation)
    • Legumes: Lentils, Chickpeas, Black beans, Kidney beans
    • Herbs and spices: Garlic, Basil, Cinnamon, Turmeric, Oregano, Rosemary





BLOOD SUGAR


  • Reduce Blood Sugar

    • Non-Starchy Vegetables: Leafy greens (spinach, kale, lettuce), Broccoli, Cauliflower, Bell peppers, Cucumber, Brussels sprouts, Zucchini
    • Whole Grains: Brown rice, Quinoa, Oats, Barley, Whole wheat bread (in moderation)
    • Lean Proteins: Chicken breast, Turkey breast, Fish (salmon, tuna, mackerel), Tofu, Lentils, Beans (black beans, kidney beans)
    • Healthy Fats: Avocado, Olive oil, Nuts (almonds, walnuts, pistachios), Seeds (chia seeds, flaxseeds)
    • Fruits (in moderation due to natural sugars): Berries (blueberries, strawberries, raspberries), Citrus fruits (oranges, grapefruits), Apples, Pears
    • Dairy (choose low-fat options): Greek yogurt (unsweetened), Cottage cheese (low-fat), Skim milk
    • Herbs and Spices: Cinnamon, Turmeric, Garlic, Ginger, Fenugreek
    • Beverages: Water, Green tea (unsweetened), Herbal teas (such as chamomile or peppermint)
